# Prompt Engineering Task 1 - Prompt Collection

## Prompt 1: Homepage Copy

Act as a professional website copywriter.

Create website homepage copy for:

Business Name: Bright Future Academy
Location: Hyderabad

About:
A coaching institute helping students prepare for IIT-JEE, NEET, and Foundation programs.

Target Audience:
Students of Classes 8–12 and their parents.

Generate:

1. Headline
2. Sub-headline
3. Introduction paragraph
4. Why Choose Us section
5. Student Success section
6. Final CTA

Write in a professional, trustworthy, and motivating tone.
